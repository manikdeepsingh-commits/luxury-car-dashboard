🧾 Cayenne Ownership Notes for Ottawa

✅ Winter Performance
Cayenne AWD is excellent in Ottawa winters. 335hp V6 + Porsche AWD handles snow and ice well. No storage concerns — full 4-season daily driver.
⚠️ No Local Dealer
Closest Porsche dealer to Ottawa: Porsche Centre Ottawa (Barrhaven area). Verify service/warranty support before buying a GTA unit. Delivery costs ~$1,000–$1,500 to Ottawa.
💡 Maintenance Budget
Cayenne maintenance: $2,000–$3,500/yr at Porsche dealer. Oil changes ~$350–$450. Budget higher than BMW or Mercedes equivalent. Factor into TCO.
💎 Prestige Factor
The Cayenne is universally recognized as the most prestigious SUV in budget. Porsche badge commands respect that Mercedes or BMW at same price cannot match. Residual values hold well.
