Mid-engined 2-seat roadster — the purest driving experience in the Porsche lineup. Best: 2020 manual at 16,500 km in Orléans (16km!) · June 2026 · AutoTrader.ca

❄️
Ottawa Winter Reality Check
The 718 Boxster is a rear-wheel-drive convertible with a low, wide stance. It is not suitable as a primary vehicle in Ottawa where you'll see 4+ months of snow, ice, and road salt. If you buy a Boxster, plan to garage it October–April and have a second car (or assume Uber costs). That said — for 5 months of pure driving bliss, it's arguably the most rewarding car on this list.

🧾 Boxster Ownership Notes for Ottawa

✅ Summer/Fall Experience
Ottawa has genuinely beautiful driving roads — Gatineau Park, Champlain Bridge, Highway 50. A Boxster here May–October is incredible. The soft-top folds in 9 seconds at speeds up to 50km/h.
❌ Winter Storage
RWD + convertible = winter storage mandatory. Budget $150–$300/mo for indoor heated storage (Oct–Apr). Or: separate winter car (Civic, RAV4) + Boxster seasonally.
⚠️ IMS / Intermediate Shaft
2017+ 718 uses a 4-cyl engine — IMS bearing issues of older 981 don't apply. Reliability is good. Main concern: coolant temp sensor, PCV, and convertible top hydraulics on high-km cars.
💡 Manual vs PDK
Manual is rarer (and better for driving fun). PDK is faster in lap times. The 2020 Orléans listing is manual — that alone makes it special. Manual Porsches hold value better at resale.
